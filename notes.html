Class 1: Electricty and First Circuit

    Circuit Rules of thumb:

    • Do not: connect + and - terminals directly to each other (i.e. Short circut)
    • Do: connect components that use all your voltage (resistors, LEDs, Motors, etc)
    • Circuits must be closed in order for electricity to flow
    • Switches control the opening and closing of your circuit
    • Switches are just conductive materials that can either open or close
